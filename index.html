Who are we?

Crossroad is a family-based, vibrant church in Seaton, Devon that caters for everyone from nought to ninety.

We offer a warm welcome to visitors whether you are on holiday, recently moved to the area or “just looking”. The services are relaxed and open but with a vibrancy and joy which we want to express in our worship to God. So whatever your age or background you will be at home with us.

What's On?

Sunday Mornings

Our family service starts at 10.30 am and finishes at 11.15 am, and we also have an online service for those not able to attend in person.

Homegroups

We have a number of homegroups throughout the week in Seaton and the surrounding area. If you would like to join one of our groups get in touch with us.

Prayer

Prayer is the heart beat of our church. If you would like personal prayer, please contact us. We would be delighted to pray for you.

Charity & Misson

At Crossroad we love to support missions and charities in the UK and across the world. We focus much of our support in Nicaragua, Uganda and the Philippines.
